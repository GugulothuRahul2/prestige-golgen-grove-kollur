About Prestige Golden Grove

Prestige Golden Grove is a futuristic luxury residential project in Tellapur, West Hyderabad. Offering 2.5, 3, 3.5 & 4 BHK modern apartments with sweeping city views, green spaces, and contemporary architecture. Located near Financial District and Outer Ring Road (ORR) with seamless connectivity to Gachibowli, HITEC City, and Kokapet.

The project is designed with world-class amenities, sustainable features, and smart home technologies to provide residents with a premium lifestyle experience in Hyderabad's most promising residential hub.
